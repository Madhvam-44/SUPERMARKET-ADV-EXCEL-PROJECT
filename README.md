🛒 Supermarket Sales Dashboard

1.  Overview

This dashboard provides a 360° view of supermarket sales performance, helping stakeholders quickly answer questions like:

Which branch generates the most orders?
Which product lines drive the highest revenue?
How does product preference differ by gender?
How do sales trend over time?
How are customers rating different store locations?

2.   Key Metrics (KPIs)

Total Orders - 1,000
Total Revenue - 322,966.75

3. Dashboard Components


No. of Orders by Branch – Bar chart comparing order volume across Branch A, B, and C
Total Revenue by Product Line – Revenue breakdown across 6 product categories (Electronic Accessories, Fashion Accessories, Food & Beverages, Health & Beauty, Home & Lifestyle, Sports & Travel)
Product Line Popularity by Gender – Side-by-side comparison of male vs. female purchases per product line
Timeline Chart – Daily invoice trend across the reporting period (Jan–Mar 2019)
Average Rating by City – Donut chart comparing customer satisfaction across Mandalay, Naypyitaw, and Yangon
Interactive Slicers – Filter the entire dashboard by Gender, Branch, Payment Method, Customer Type, Product Line, and Date (with quarterly timeline control)


4. Tools & Technologies


Microsoft Excel – PivotTables, PivotCharts, Slicers, Timeline filter
Data source: Supermarket sales transaction dataset (Invoice ID, Branch, City, Customer Type, Gender, Product Line, Unit Price, Quantity, Total, Date, Payment, Rating)


5.  Key Insights


Branch A leads all locations in number of orders
Food & Beverages and Sports & Travel are the top revenue-generating product lines
Fashion Accessories and Electronic Accessories show strong popularity among female customers, while Health & Beauty skews toward male customers
Customer ratings are fairly evenly distributed across all three cities (~32–34% each)

6.  How to Use


Clone or download this repository
Open Supermarket_Sales_Dashboard.xlsx in Microsoft Excel
Use the slicers on the right panel to filter by Gender, Branch, Payment type, Customer type, Product line, or Date
Explore the charts to uncover sales trends and patterns
